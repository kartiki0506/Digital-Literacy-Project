# Stay Safe Online — Cybercrime Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

---

## 🔒 General Safety

1. Never click on unknown links or download attachments from 
   unverified emails or messages.
2. Always download software from official websites only — 
   avoid cracked or pirated software.
3. Keep your device's operating system and antivirus software 
   updated regularly.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for every account and enable 
   two-factor authentication (2FA).
5. Always back up your important files on Google Drive or an 
   external hard drive regularly.

---

## 💰 Financial & UPI Safety

6. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone 
   — even if they claim to be from your bank.
7. Always verify the receiver's UPI ID carefully before making 
   any payment — scammers use similar looking IDs.

---

## 📱 Device & Account Safety

8. Never connect to unknown or public WiFi networks without 
   using a VPN.
9. Log out of your accounts on shared or public computers 
   after use.
10. Immediately report any suspicious activity to the National 
    Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call helpline 
    1930 (available 24x7).
