# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
Canva (canva.com)

## What My Design Shows
This infographic covers three key topics of digital literacy:
- What digital literacy means for students
- Safe internet practices
- Professional online presence

## What I Found Interesting
One thing I found particularly interesting while making this infographic was how much thought goes into visual hierarchy — deciding what the viewer reads first and what stands out most. Choosing the right colours and icons to match each section was more challenging than I expected, but the final result clearly communicates the key ideas of digital literacy in a simple and engaging format.
